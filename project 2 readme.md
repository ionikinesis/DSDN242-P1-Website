HOW YOU PLAY
during the day: sheep will spawn until you have the max capacity sheep your farm can have. 
during the night: the god will appear, drag your sheep onto the altar to sacrifice them in exchange you get crystals
during the day: you can exchange the crystals for gold, with which you can use to upgrade your farm or build and upgrade a temple dedicated to the god.
(debug mode added for marking)
